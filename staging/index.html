Arco da Rua Augusta

This triumphant arch connects Rua Augusta to the impressive Praça do Comércio. Climb to the top for panoramic views of downtown Lisbon and the Tagus River.

Learn more
STAGING

Tram 28

Hop on this iconic yellow tram for a scenic journey through Lisbon's historic neighborhoods. The route passes by many major attractions and offers a glimpse into local life.

Learn more
STAGING

Alfama District

Lose yourself in Lisbon's oldest neighborhood, a labyrinth of narrow streets, ancient houses, and hidden squares. Experience authentic Fado music in local taverns.

Learn more
STAGING

São Jorge Castle

Perched atop Lisbon's highest hill, this ancient castle offers panoramic views of the city. Explore the archaeological site and walk along the medieval walls.

Learn more
STAGING

LX Factory

Once an industrial complex, now a creative hub filled with shops, restaurants, and art spaces. Visit on Sunday for the weekly market featuring local artisans and vintage finds.

Learn more
STAGING

Pastéis de Belém

Visit the original bakery that has been making the famous Pastéis de Nata since 1837. These delicious custard tarts are a culinary icon of Portugal and a must-try delicacy.
